ACA Global Scholars Program

ACA International’s premier summer experience offers six weeks of academic exploration, creative engagement, and real-world problem solving for students in GK–G8.

2025 Focus: NextGen STEM — Empowering Tomorrow’s Innovators

From artificial intelligence to sustainability, students will build the skills, tools, and mindsets they need to succeed in a rapidly evolving world.

Program Structure
  • Location: ACA International Campus, Seoul
  • Grades: GK–G8
  • Dates: June 16 – July 25, 2025
  • Schedule: 6 weeks with 3 themed sessions
  • Daily Time: 8:30 AM – 3:40 PM (Extended Day available)
Program Highlights
  • STEM Labs and Maker Projects
  • Inquiry-Based Literacy & ELA
  • Capstone Projects and Exhibitions
  • Leadership and Creative Arts Activities
  • Guest Speaker Series
  • Extended Day Options (Advanced CS, UN Book Club)

2025 Sessions & Themes

Session 1: Invent the Future

June 16 – June 27

Explore innovation, design thinking, and entrepreneurship through hands-on STEM challenges and maker activities.

Session 2: Science for a Changing World

June 30 – July 11

Investigate biology, sustainability, and climate change. Develop critical thinking and global responsibility.

Session 3: The Future of Humanity

July 14 – July 25

Examine AI, ethics, and digital society. Learn how to create purposeful and human-centered technology.
